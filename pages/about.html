Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Ryan and welcome to my website. I have a background in chemical engineering and solution engineering, with a strong foundation in wet-lab neuroscience surrounding microglia and macrophage cells. Currently, I'm exploring the intersection between neuroscience and engineering, specifically how we can take advantage of AI to further our understanding of the field. As a part of this, I'm testing with ChatGPT to achieve this goal. In my free time, I'm really into tennis and disc golf, and I also enjoy hiking, playing the piano, and keeping up with the latest developments in neuroscience research.
